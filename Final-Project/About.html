About Spartans For The Cure

THE POWER OF PINK AND GREEN COMBINED, SAVING ONE CUP AT A TIME.

Whether you're looking to meet new people and make friends, volunteer and make a difference, or just have fun, Spartans for the Cure has it all. We welcome both guys and girls to join our club and come together for a great cause!

Spartans for the Cure hosts and participates in a variety of activities including:

  • Sparticipation
  • MSU Homecoming Parade
  • Go Green Wear Pink (Facebook/GoGreenWearPink)
  • Bake sales
  • Yoplait Save Lids to Save Lives Campaign
  • Holiday card writing
  • Bowl for the Cure
  • Race for the Cure
  • Bra Crawl
  • Ride for the Cure

Our Mission

Spartans for the Cure™ exists to spread breast cancer awareness throughout Michigan State University’s campus and the community of East Lansing. The organization utilizes its strong student base to act as advocates in the fight to end breast cancer by educating others on the facts of breast health, engaging the community through a variety of events, and bettering the lives of those touched by breast cancer.
